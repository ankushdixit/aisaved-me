## 4. Automated Privacy Protection (PII Redaction)

### 4.1 How It Works

We use AI-powered systems (Google Gemini Pro) to automatically detect and redact personally identifiable information (PII) from submitted content before publication. This includes:

- Names of individuals (other than public figures)
- Addresses and location details
- Phone numbers and email addresses
- Financial account numbers
- Social Security numbers and government IDs
- Medical record numbers
- Other sensitive personal identifiers

### 4.2 Limitations

- Automated redaction may not catch all PII
- You should review your published content
- Contact us immediately if you notice unredacted sensitive information
- We will promptly address any privacy concerns

### 4.3 Your Responsibility

While we provide automated protection, you are responsible for:

- Not including unnecessary sensitive information in submissions
- Reviewing published content for accuracy
- Reporting any privacy concerns promptly
